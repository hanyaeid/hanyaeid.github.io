Hi!

My name is Han :)

I'm a Computer Science and Math major at MUN. I moved to Newfoundland when i was 17 to initially pursue Mechanical Engineering.

While it wasn't the right fit of a degree for me, I continue to enjoy and exercise concepts of mechanical design and construction daily through my job at the university's makerspace and 3D printing lab.

I created this website to serve as a digital archive for my life [a Harchive if you will]. I hope this will motivate me to carve out time to do the things I enjoy more, and also help me appreciate the beauty in the everyday by documenting it.

In this Harchive, you'll find everything from my professional portfolio, to my favorite media and recipes I'm working on. I hope you enjoy this glimpse into my life!
