General

This is pretty much a personal diary. No rhyme, no reason, just one post
a day to remember the special moments when they naturally strike, and to seek them
out if they don't.

Beauty is all around us, and here is where you would watch me discover that :)

6th of Jan, 2024

quiet day

Today's weather was infinitely worst than yesterdays - I'll take any amount of non-windy snowfall over 70km/hr winds anyday. I made the executive decision to stay home all day and eat cereal and frozen fruit. I was feeling a bit blue in the evening, so decided to watch Derry Girls for a pick me up.
I realized after that I was probably feeling blue because I ate over a pound of frozen blueberries throughout the day (no joke). I also discovered that doing so gives you some funny colored sh*ts and stains your fingers for a day after, despite washing :) it reminded me of the time I consumed a 10lb bag of beets throughout a 3 week interval and had the most vibrant pisses of my life (thank you beeturia)

5th of Jan, 2024

winter wonderland: first snow day!

First snow of the semester on the first week of classes (second day too) is divine intervention. The break was way too short, so I'm grateful for the opportunity to slowly wind back.
Despite the perfect opportunity for an indoors day, I went for a walk in the evening and it was kind of cute. I love feeling like the lone survivor in a post-apocalyptic society (genuinely).

4th of Jan, 2024

first day of classes

First day back to the grind, today was all about the small wins. I woke up feeling gross and frail but went to work/class anyways (the temptation to skip was mammoth). The weather sucked today as we're gearing up for a weekend storm, and I forgot to take a picture today....
On the bright side, my friend Gracie sent me this meme, and it made me smile. Of course because it's hilarious, but mostly because I saw it the night before and meant to send it to her but didn't. The connection is too real to deny - we were destined to sit on a sandwouch™ together, maybe even share a tiny sandwich while we're there. What a life.

3rd of Jan, 2024

fun coffee + cute cat

Found this lil' guy taking a sip of my coffee this morning ^_^ Of course I had to report him for theft, but that's a different saga :/
Molly was being incredibly sweet this morning, she's perched perfectly across Jenn's back for maximal surface-area contact™ , I couldn't not include it.

2nd of Jan, 2024

best cats (and friends) ever

I got to stay over with my friends Jenn and Amnesty, which is always a treat :) They have the comfiest couch on the planet I'm pretty sure.
I got to help them clean for the new year (which is *awesome* because now I know where everything is ;] ), and then we played Priorities (very fun board game) and a couple rounds of Contexto and Nerdle before going to bed.
They have the sweetest cats ever, Molly joined me for the whole night on the couch. I woke up the next day to both Molly and Nancy asserting dominance over their territory and saying good morning <3

1st of Jan, 2024

first chore of the year

Nothing like starting the new year with some productivity. I spent the day today deep cleaning my house, and did a very large, very overdue, load of laundry in record time.
I hate laundry, and I never get it done in one day. But I got this out of the dryer and folded in under an hour...not bad!
Although....I still haven't put it away. It's on the designated laundry chair, hopefully I can get around to it before next year #wrinklefree2024

31st of Dec, 2023

happy new year!!

I don't really celebrate the new year, and this year is no exception. I was going to have a night of indoors reflecting and ruminating (2 of my favorite hobbies) but decided last minute to go to my friends Sam and Lee's houseparty. I caught some pretty cool fireworks on the way, and got to spend some good time with friends. Not a bad night :) #rip2023

30th of Dec, 2023

first post + first snowfall!!

First proper snowfall of the season has a sort of novelty to it, so it had to be celebrated :)
This year, I am making an effort to shovel more regularly. I had a really good time with it last night, everything was perfect! The snow was my favorite consistency: fluffy, slightly icy, and wet (but not sloppy). My favorite thing about it was the moderation, I think this is the perfect amount of snow for all season round festivities, no one who lives in a city needs any more than that. The air was also so crisp and fresh, and I managed to shovel in an almost straight line. Pretty neat :)
